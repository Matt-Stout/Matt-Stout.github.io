About Me

I am a Software and Front-End Web Developer currently studying at the University of Wisconsin – Madison for a BA in Computer Science and a Certificate in Digital Studies. I have worked on personal web development projects, and have a year of experience in software development for a telecommunications company.

I am looking to improve my design and UX/UI skills. I love working with and keeping up with the latest technologies, always willing to learn more. From finance and marketing applications to web and mobile development, I constantly push myself to escalate my skills and knowledge.

Although I am not a graphic designer specifically, I do photography and editing in my free time. This gives me an edge when it comes to the design aspect of web development. It gives me the creative eye necessary to provide exceptional aesthetics and visual appeal, while my technology background keeps me grounded on efficient interface usability.
